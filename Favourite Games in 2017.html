Destiny 2

Destiny 2 provides a robust shooter-RPG experience to gamers on the Xbox One, PS4, and PC platforms. Destiny 2 is the sequel to the hit game, Destiny. Continue the story as Earth's guardians and take back our galaxy from the Red Legion.

Release Date: September 6, 2017

Platform: PlayStation 4, Xbox One, Microsoft Windows

Developer: Bungie
